About Devcation '25

Devcation is a week-long technical festival organized by Google Developer Groups – Indira Gandhi Delhi Technical University for Women. It celebrates diversity in the TechForGood domain. The week features Devcation ‘25 Hackathon, filled with events that fuel creativity, foster innovation, and help you craft tech solutions that make an impact.

About GDG IGDTUW

user@gdg:~$ We explore tech and build solutions for a better world 🌍

🚀 GDG IGDTUW is a student-led chapter of Google Developer Groups, focusing on building a strong tech community within the university.

🎯 Being part of a women-centric university, GDG IGDTUW strongly promotes the participation and empowerment of women in STEM fields.

💡 Regular workshops, coding bootcamps, and hackathons are conducted to enhance practical skills in areas like Android development, web, machine learning.

🚀 7000+ students have expanded their knowledge through our community.

💡 Expert talks from industry professionals and Googlers expose students to real-world tech insights and career guidance.

🎯 Focus on Google technologies such as Firebase, Flutter, TensorFlow, and Google Cloud Platform.

💡 Networking opportunities with peers, alumni, and professionals through events and collaborative projects.

🚀 Members participate in global GDG initiatives like DevFest, Solution Challenge, and Women Techmakers.
